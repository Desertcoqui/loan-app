Gryffindor Loan App


This Loan App will estimate your monthly payments and also tell you how much interest you will pay through the term of the loan. Here you will have three fields of input which the app will need to determine your payments. Loan Amount/Principal, Interest Rate and the Number of Years. Unlike the other houses, we have built our loan calculator through man years of wizardly knowledge. Using our loan app will give you the powers you need to concur all your financial goals while continuing to be a faithful member of the wizardly house of Gryffindor!!

Through the past two hundred years the loan app has helped many in their paths to greatness, without these calculations many of our students and staff would not know where to turn in times of need. There have many many attempts in the past to steal the loan application with no success. We encourage all our faculty and young wizards to use the loan applications as often as possible as this strengthen the application and makes it very difficult for the other houses to take over it. In the past we have had a few close calls , but thanks to the relentless powers of our young wizards we have prevailed in keeping the integrity of the loan app working. It is just a matter of time before the dark forces descend upon the calculator and try to claim it as their own. Your faithful usage of the Gryffindor loan app will continue to keep it protected from those dark forces.
